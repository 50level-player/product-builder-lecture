About AI Animal Face Test

The Project

AI Animal Face Test is a fun, interactive web application designed to help you discover which "animal face type" you possess. By leveraging the latest advancements in Artificial Intelligence, we provide a playful yet sophisticated way to analyze facial features and categorize them into popular archetypes like the "Dog Face" or "Cat Face."

Our goal is to create an engaging experience that combines technology, psychology, and pop culture. Whether you're curious about your own "vibe" or just want to have fun with friends, our AI is here to help you decode your look.

The Technology: Google Teachable Machine

At the heart of our service is Google Teachable Machine, a powerful platform that allows us to train machine learning models to recognize patterns in images. Our model has been meticulously trained on a diverse dataset of facial images, learning to distinguish the subtle nuances that define different animal-like facial structures.

By using TensorFlow.js, we bring this machine learning model directly to your web browser. This means all the processing happens on your device in real-time. Your camera feed is never sent to our servers, ensuring that your privacy is protected while providing a seamless, high-performance experience.

Our Purpose

We believe that AI should be accessible, transparent, and enjoyable. This project serves as a demonstration of how modern AI can be used for entertainment and education. By explaining the mechanics behind our tool, we hope to demystify AI and show its potential in everyday applications.
