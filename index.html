What are Animal Face Types?

In various cultures, facial features are often compared to animals to describe a person's "vibe" or personality. The most popular comparison is between "Dog Face" and "Cat Face" types. This isn't about looking like an actual animal, but rather the energy and structural impressions your face gives off.

Characteristics of Dog Faces vs. Cat Faces

Dog Face Type: Generally characterized by rounder eyes, a softer jawline, and a friendly, approachable expression. People with dog-like features are often perceived as warm, loyal, and energetic.

Cat Face Type: Characterized by sharper, slightly upturned eyes, a more defined bone structure, and a sophisticated or mysterious expression. Cat-like features often give off a "chic," independent, and elegant vibe.

How AI Image Recognition Works

Our tool uses Teachable Machine, a web-based tool by Google that makes creating machine learning models fast, easy, and accessible to everyone. Here's the process:

  • Data Collection: We trained the model using thousands of images labeled as 'Dog-like' or 'Cat-like'.
  • Neural Networks: The AI uses deep learning to identify patterns in facial structures, such as eye shape, nose position, and overall face contour.
  • Probability Analysis: When you show your face, the model calculates the probability of your features matching the patterns it learned during training.

This technology brings the power of TensorFlow.js directly to your browser, meaning your image data is processed locally and never uploaded to a server, ensuring your privacy.
